About Us

Mindful Pastures is a online mental health resource hub where you can find guided meditations, encouragement in your mental health journey, and tips on managing your mental health. We also have links under our Therapy page to help you find a therapist or support groups.


Our Mission

We are here to help you find healthy coping strategies and to be a place that encourages you to overcome any obstacles you may face regarding mental health. We also provide resources to help you find therapists and support groups. However, we also realize and want to enforce that we are not in any way a replacement for professional help. Our overall mission is to provide resources to help you live a healthy life.
